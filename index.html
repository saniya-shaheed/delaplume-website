About

Inspired by a lot of calligraphy collections from social media, Saniya shaheed continues the long historic tradition of hand-painting calligraphy. Taking a mathematical approach to applying each design, each of them is divided and mapped out by eye to highlight their tapering forms, resulting in a contemporary take on the much loved, and often nostalgic combination of colours.
Saniya shaheed is currently residing in UAE, continuing her artworks by creating new stories between colours, canvas and brushes.
